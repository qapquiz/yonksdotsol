# Privacy Policy for Yonks

**Effective Date: March 1, 2026**

Yonks ("we", "our", or "us") is a decentralized application built on the Solana blockchain. We value your privacy and aim to be transparent about how data is handled.

## 1. Information Collection

- **Personal Data:** We do not collect, store, or process any personal identification information (such as names, emails, or phone numbers).
- **On-Chain Data:** By using this application, your public wallet address and transaction history on the Solana blockchain are publicly visible. This is a fundamental characteristic of blockchain technology.

## 2. Information Usage

- We do not sell or share any user data.
- Any data "collected" is limited to technical logs required to facilitate connection between the app and the Solana network via your chosen wallet provider.

## 3. Third-Party Services

Yonks interacts with third-party wallet providers (e.g., Phantom, Solflare) and the Solana network. Your use of these services is governed by their respective privacy policies.

## 4. Security

We do not have access to your private keys or seed phrases. You are solely responsible for the security of your wallet.

## 5. Contact

For any questions regarding this policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository: https://github.com/qapquiz/yonksdotsol
